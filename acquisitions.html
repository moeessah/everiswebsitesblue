Our Process

Simple, transparent, fast.

From first contact to close, we keep it straightforward.

1
24-48 hours

First Response

Rob or Liam reads every message personally. No intake forms, no gatekeepers: just a real conversation with decision-makers.

2
1-2 weeks

Initial Discovery

We learn about your business, your goals, and what matters most to you. Share what you're comfortable sharing. We'll respect your confidentiality.

3
2-4 weeks

Due Diligence

We do our homework efficiently. We focus on what matters: the business fundamentals, the team, and the fit. No unnecessary friction.

4
1-2 weeks

Term Sheet & Close

If it's a match, we move quickly. Clean term sheets, straightforward agreements, and a closing process that respects your time.

After Close

Your brand stays exactly as your customers know it. The logo, the name, the product identity. All preserved.

Team Continuity

Invested Growth

We invest in the product, the team, and the customer experience. We're here to help it thrive for years to come.
